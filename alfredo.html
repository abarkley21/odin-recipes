Delicious Chicken Alfredo

A recipe of a homemade chicken and alfredo past dish.

Ingredients

  • Alfredo Pasta
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chicken
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken in a pan
  2. Combine parmesan, heavy cream, butter into another pan on medium heat
  3. Once combined, add chicken
  4. Boil water and add broccoli
  5. Once cooked add all together and salt and pepper to taste

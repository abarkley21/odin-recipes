Delicious Donuts

A recipe of a homemade donuts.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Heavy Cream
  • Eggs
  • Corn Syrup

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Let the dough rise
  3. Punch down the dough to release the air
  4. Roll and cut into donuts
  5. Fry the donuts

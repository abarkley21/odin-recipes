Delicious Spaghetti

A recipe of a homemade spaghetti dish.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Ragu
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Beef

Steps

  1. Cook the beef in a pan
  2. Heat up Ragu
  3. Add Ragu to the cooked beef
  4. Boil water and cook pasta
  5. Once cooked add all together and salt and pepper to taste
